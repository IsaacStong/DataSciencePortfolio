Data Analytics

My love for data analytics started my junior year of college when I took Intro to Data Science. I believe machine learning has the power to automate a lot of business decisions and create a data driven company. I am also passionate about machine learning as it holds the ability to create more data from images and natural text. NLP and Image Recognition are two areas that really interest me and I hope to learn more about them as my career develops.

Engineering
